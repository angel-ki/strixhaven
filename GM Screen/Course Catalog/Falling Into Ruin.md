### Falling Into Ruin: Guidelines on Successful Exploration of Historical Sites
Associated Skills: Athletics, History, Sleight of Hand This class is taught by Professor Rosaline Fingali. In this introductory class Rosaline teaches students to be dumb enough to head into ruins but smart enough to know when to get out and any who pass learn how to cast Expeditious Retreat.
