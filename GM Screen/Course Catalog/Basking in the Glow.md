### Basking in the Glow: Basic Magical Auras
Associated Skills: Arcana, Investigation, Perception 
This class is led by a vedalken named Odyssey. In this class they teach students about the patterns left behind by different schools of magic and encourages them to look for those patterns in the higher level spells they'll eventually go on to learn. As a reward for passing this class students are taught to cast Detect Magic
