### Background
As a relatively small individual, even among fairies, Clem has always been concerned with their size. Standing at just one and a half feet tall, with lilac wings that resemble flower petals, Clem was often bullied. Frustrated and hurt, Clem left the feywilds at a young age and wandered from town to town until she received an invitation from Strixhaven. Infused with the same magic as other fairies, Clem has always been able to make herself slightly larger. But by studying Quandrix magic, she learned to use a special, self made theorem. This allows her to cast the Enlarge spell on herself multiple times without maintaining concentration on each individual spell. With this at her disposal, Clem can stack applications and grow to an enormous size. Clem has a zero-tolerance policy for bullies. If she encounters one, they’ve been known to use her ability to make instigators feel as small as their intended victims. Clem has a soft spot for the underdog and often uses magic to help small critters and plants grow into larger versions of themselves.
