### Background
- A permanently wild-shaped druid who discovered how to communicate with humans in her bear form
	- Due to an alchemical accident, she became trapped in this form as a student. This accident could have been prevented if she had been properly trained.
	- She is passionate about keeping her students alive and loves to teach introductory courses for this reason
- She works under the Witherbloom college and practices zoomancy in her free time (divination based on animal behavior). Her true skills lie in alchemy and crafting potions that summon the nearest monsters of a certain type
