Background
- Quandrix student
- She is a ticket taker for the Strixhaven Arts Society
- She is a member of the Intramural Water-Dancing Club
- She has a natural affinity for aquatic creatures and is working on a solo routine with a school of fish. Rehearsals aren't going well.
- She has ADHD. It's not that she forgets about her commitments, she just gets distracted. She spends more time learning her routines than anyone else because she constantly loses focus.
- She might be a Quandrix student, but she's only good at math when she's able to pay attention. Sometimes, Indi helps with this. Unfortunately, she just had to switch her job involving giving the correct change to people because she kept on making silly mistakes.
- She seeks to become a vivifier and conjure animals from fractals that she can see, hear, and communicate with other animals through.
### Personality Traits
- Equations and patterns come naturally to my mind. I wish friendship came just as easily.
### Ideals
- Sincerity. There's no good in pretending to be something I'm not.
### Bonds

### Flaws
