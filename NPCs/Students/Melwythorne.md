Background
- Witherbloom student
- Member of the Intramural Silkball Club and Student Mages of Faith
- Lives a long life span and speaks and moves slowly. Human time does not make sense to him.
- Very calm even as other students panic.
- He had never left his grove before attending Strixhaven and the close-knit ISC has helped him recover from homesickness.
- His grove always accepted him even after he came out as trans.
- Believes that nature and all living beings form a consciousness extending throughout the planes of existence and beyond the powers of any one deity
### Personality Traits
- Villages, towns, and cities do not suit me. I'd rather be out in the wilderness any day.
### Ideals
- Belonging. All creatures have a place in the world, so I strive to help others find theirs.
### Bonds
- I ache to return to my tribe and the family I left, but cannot until my obligations are fulfilled.
### Flaws
- Most people scream and run when they see a demon. I stop and take notes on its anatomy.
