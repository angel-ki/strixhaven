Background
- Second year Prismari student
- Member of the Distinguished Society of Fine Arts
- Works as an RA in the dorms with Azure
- She is trans and is always ready to offer her support to other queer students.
- She spends much of her free time working on her pottery. Despite her enthusiasm, her technical skill is lacking. She still makes mug after mug and frequently gifts them to friends.
### Personality Traits
- It doesn't matter if the whole world's against me. I'll always do what I think is right.
### Ideals
- Honesty. Art should reflect the soul; it should come from within and reveal who we really are.
### Bonds
- I didn't gain acceptance easily. So, I will always be there for those who need to be seen for who they are.
### Flaws
- Instead of confronting my negative emotions, I channel them into explosive artistic displays.
